Welcome

You probably got here because you are interested in a multiplayer RPG gamemode for Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas

How do you do it?
Some very talented people made a modification for GTA:SA called MTA that let's people play the game multiplayer.
Furthermore, they provide hooks to let the server hosts add functionality to the game by running Lua code.
Have a look at their website: https://www.mtasa.com/

What are you making?
"We" are me (Luca aka Anirudh Katoch, India), Carmelo (Italy) and Victor (Brazil).
We are making a server that would allow players to engage in "real-life"-like gameplay scenario, with roles, events and specific situations they can freely adhere to.
Players would be required to make a character. The state and information of the character is saved across each play.
For example, a player that leans towards police-type activities actually becomes a cop, and receives the power and freedom to arrest criminals, investigate crimes, intercept vehicle chases or even stop a bank robbery.
Criminals may team up with other criminals, commit robberies, join a gang, and conquer their own turfs / neighborhoods.
Players who lean more towards civilian activities mainly engage in economy-oriented work, develop their own businesses and strive to grow in influence within the server economy.
Previous servers have always either stuck to the "way too simple and primitive", or the "deep, yet overly complex" format. Here, we are going for something new : Attempting to get the best of both, and making something deep, with extreme freedom, and at the same time, easy to pick up and understand.
That's right. You choose and build your own path.
The server would be free to play, and making money is not our aim.
I think you can imagine the kind of gameplay we are aiming for. We call it RPG, or light roleplay.

How can I help?
If you have the relevant skills, you can help us develop this project.
Otherwise, you can play on the server once it's done :)
